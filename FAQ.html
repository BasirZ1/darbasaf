Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is your app about?

Our app connects users based on location, allowing them to send and receive voice messages (shouts) in real time. It helps users find people in specific areas for work, socializing, or business purposes.

When will the Apple version be available?

The Apple version is currently under development and will be available soon. Follow us on social media for updates!

How do I report a problem?

If you encounter any issues, feel free to contact our support team at support@example.com or through the help section in the app.

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes, you can log into your account on multiple devices as a passenger. However, for drivers, your WebSocket session is used to maintain activity, so be cautious of switching devices during active trips.

Is there a subscription fee?

No, our app is free to use. However, certain premium features may be introduced in the future.
