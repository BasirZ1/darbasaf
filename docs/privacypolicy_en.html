Darbas & Co. Privacy Policy

Last Updated: Aug 4, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Darbas & Co. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services. By using our services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information: Includes name, email address, and phone number.

Location Data: We collect location information when you use the app to request or provide rides.

Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including logs of activities and usage patterns.

Device Information: Information about your device, including device ID, operating system, and app version.

3. How We Use Your Information

To Provide Services: To facilitate ride requests and provide transportation services.

To Improve Our Services: To analyze usage data and enhance our app's functionality.

To Communicate with You: To send notifications, updates, and promotional information related to our services.

To Ensure Safety and Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent activities and ensure the safety of users.

4. How We Share Your Information

With Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our services.

For Legal Reasons: If required by law, we may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.

With Your Consent: We may share your information with your consent or at your direction.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.

Correction: You can request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information.

Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal information, subject to legal and contractual obligations.

Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving promotional communications from us.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy in the app or via email.
